## Role Description

The **Gaslighter** is a Neutral Evil role that has varying abilities each round. The ability cycle and their effects are as follows:

- **Round 1:** Normal Kill.
- **Round 2:** **Knight** people, granting the target an extra vote in the next meeting.
- **Round 3:** **Curse** a player. If the Gaslighter is not ejected during the next meeting, the cursed player dies.
- **Round 4:** **Shield** players, stopping a kill attempt against them.

**Related Roles:** [**Monarch**](../../../crewmate/support/monarch), [**Witch**](../../../impostor/killing/witch), [**Medic**](../../../crewmate/support/medic)
