## Role Description

The **Opportunist** is a Neutral Benign role. The Opportunist's win condition is simply to survive until the end of the game to win with the winning team. The Opportunist may gain immunity to normal attacks after finishing tasks if configured to do so. Many roles can become Opportunist in specific cases.
