As the Puppeteer, you can use your kill button to manipulate another player. This player will become *puppeted*. After a configurable amount of time, the *puppeted* player will become *manipulated* and kill any player that enters their killing range **except** players with the Lazy Guy role, players with the Lazy add-on, or other Impostors, including the Puppeteer themself (unless configured otherwise).

A layer of difficulty can be added to the role by configuring a lower maximum number of puppets the Puppeteer can make, or having the Puppeteer die after using all of their allocated puppet abilities. There is also an option to have the *puppeted* player die with their victim. 

## Options

<RoleOption number={{default: 1}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Max
  </Fragment>
  The cap for how many of this role can spawn.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption time={{default: 22.5}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Puppet Cooldown
  </Fragment>
  The amount of time after the start of each round until the Puppeteer can use their puppet ability.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: true}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Can Kill Normally
  </Fragment>
  Whether the Puppeteer can kill normally. If enabled, the Puppeteer must press the kill button twice to perform a kill.

  <Fragment slot='sub-options'>
    <RoleOption time={{default: 25}}>
      <Fragment slot='title'>
        #### Puppeteer Kill Cooldown
      </Fragment>
      If *Can Kill Normally* is enabled, this is the time after the start of each round until the Puppeteer can perform a kill.
    </RoleOption>
  </Fragment>
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption time={{default: 3}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Minimum Delay Before The Puppeted Player Can Kill
  </Fragment>
  The minimum delay before the *puppeted* player becomes *manipulated* and able to kill anyone who enters their range. This should never be lower than the maximum delay.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption time={{default: 7}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Maximum Delay Before The Puppeted Player Can Kill
  </Fragment>
  The maximum delay before the *puppeted* player becomes *manipulated* and able to kill anyone who enters their range. This should never be lower than the minimum delay.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: false}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Manipulation Ends After a Specific Time
  </Fragment>
  Whether the *puppeted* player is *manipulated* indefinitely or for a limited time. If enabled, the player will return to normal after the specified duration.

  <Fragment slot='sub-options'>
    <RoleOption time={{default: 30}}>
      <Fragment slot='title'>
        #### Time After The Manipulation Ends
      </Fragment>
      The amount of time until the *manipulated* player loses the effect.  
      This timer starts **after the manipulation begins**, not after the initial puppeting.
    </RoleOption>
  </Fragment>
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: false}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Bypass Lazy
  </Fragment>
  If enabled, players with the Lazy add-on can be *puppeted*. By default, they are immune.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: false}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Bypass Lazy Guy
  </Fragment>
  If enabled, Crewmates with the Lazy Guy role can be *puppeted*. By default, they are immune.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: false}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Puppet Can Kill Other Impostors
  </Fragment>
  If enabled, the *puppeted* player can kill other impostors â€” but not the Puppeteer.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: false}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Puppet Can Kill The Puppeteer
  </Fragment>
  If enabled, the *puppeted* player can kill the Puppeteer who puppeted them.  
  Combining this with a very low [minimum delay](#minimum-delay-before-the-puppeted-player-can-kill) (0-2 seconds) can be extremely punishing for the Puppeteer.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption number={{default: 5}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Maximum Number of Puppets
  </Fragment>
  The maximum number of times the Puppeteer can puppet. Only successful puppets count, and this does not reset after meetings.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: false}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Puppeteer Dies After Running Out Of Puppets
  </Fragment>
  If enabled, the Puppeteer will die after using their last puppet. Only successful puppets count.
</RoleOption>

<RoleOption toggle={{default: false}}>
  <Fragment slot='title'>
    ### Puppet Dies Along With The Victim
  </Fragment>
  If enabled, the *puppeted* player will die along with the player they kill while manipulated.
</RoleOption>
